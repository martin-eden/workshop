Why use this instead of "table"?

This code is mostly wraps "table.insert()" and "table.move()".
Why not use them directly?

"table" functions are more powerful than we need in most our code.
They accept more arguments than we provide. In our code we want
to say "aha, it's using 'list'.." and expect less surprises from code.
